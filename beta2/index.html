Victor Maes graduated from LUCA School of Arts Brussels in 2015, and took courses in advanced cinematography at the Global Cinematography Institute in Los Angeles. He’s been working as a camera assistant on feature films and commercials (Girl, Un Ange, De Patrick) since 2016. As a cinematographer he works on various film projects, alternating between fiction and documentary as well as commercial ones. He continues to search for new ways to blend his various interests and fascinations outside film into the practice of filmmaking. His fascination for computer science, design, new media and technology drive him to search for and develop new forms that blur the boundaries between fiction, reality and animation. As he obtained a degree in film directing, he always approaches cinematography from a storytelling perspective, searching for the best way to visualise a screenplay along with the director.
