A Long Goodbye is an animated, interactive VR experience co-written and co-directed by Kate Voet and Victor Maes. Inside Ida's emptying apartment, players handle the objects and recordings of a long marriage, and each interaction draws her world and her memories of her husband Daniel vividly back to life. The experience features the voices of Elizabeth Counsel, Richard Wells, Marlies Heuer and Derek de Lint, with an original score by composer Joep Beving.

The project was produced by Cassette for Timescapes (Belgium) with Valk Productions (Netherlands) and Tarantula (Luxembourg), developed through the Venice Gap Financing Market, and supported by the Flanders Audiovisual Fund, the Creative Europe MEDIA programme, the Netherlands Film Fund and Film Fund Luxembourg, among others.

A Long Goodbye premiered in the Venice Immersive Competition at the 82nd Venice International Film Festival, where it received the Venice Immersive Achievement Prize. It went on to win the Agog Immersive Impact Award at SXSW, Best XR Film at Clermont-Ferrand, the Golden Alexander at Thessaloniki and the International XR Award at the Prix NUMIX, and continues to travel to festivals including IDFA DocLab, Film Fest Gent and Fipadoc.
